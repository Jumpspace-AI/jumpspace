# Code Of Conduct

Jumpspace should be a useful, respectful place for developers and agent-tool builders to collaborate.

## Expected Behavior

- Be direct, kind, and specific.
- Assume good intent while still naming concrete problems.
- Keep technical disagreement focused on evidence, behavior, and tradeoffs.
- Respect maintainers' time by using clear reproduction steps and truthful test results.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, hate speech, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Disruptive behavior that makes collaboration harder for others.
- Bad-faith vulnerability disclosure or attempts to trick users into unsafe commands.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and issues that violate this code. Repeated or severe violations may result in blocked participation.

Report conduct concerns privately to a maintainer through GitHub.
